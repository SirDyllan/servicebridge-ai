# Demo Video Script

Target length: 2 minutes 30 seconds.

## 0:00-0:30 - Problem and who it affects

"Public support systems can be confusing for students, young adults, families, and people with missing documents. Many users do not know the program name, which documents they need, or which office should verify their case. A person might say: I need food help, school support, and I do not have an ID. Without guidance, they can waste time visiting the wrong office or preparing the wrong documents."

Show:

- A short before moment: the user has a mixed problem, not a program name.
- Emphasize stress, confusion, and document uncertainty.

## 0:30-1:30 - What the AI does and how

"ServiceBridge AI lets the user explain the situation in plain language. The AI checks possible support pathways, identifies document readiness issues, and shows safe next steps. It does not approve benefits. It prepares the user for official verification."

Show:

- Chatbot or guided check.
- User enters the Mutare student food/school/ID scenario.
- Results page appears.
- Result cards show possible support match, documents to prepare, missing documents, next 3 steps, and source transparency.

## 1:30-2:00 - One moment where it makes a difference

"The important moment is this: the user does not just get a vague answer. They see what to prepare before visiting anyone: proof of enrollment, income-change explanation, ID alternatives, proof of residence if available, and the office type that should verify the case."

Show:

- Documents to prepare area.
- Missing documents warning.
- Next 3 steps.
- "Verify With a Human" module.

## 2:00-2:30 - Responsible AI choice and human role

"ServiceBridge AI is designed as a navigator, not a decision maker. It uses possible-match language, source labels, verification status, and a human verification step. Final eligibility and approval remain with official agencies, schools, social workers, case workers, or verified support providers."

Show:

- Disclaimer.
- Source and verification section.
- Human verification card.

## 2:30-3:00 - What we would build next

"Next, we would expand verified local records, add multilingual voice support, connect more official sources, and improve feedback loops so incorrect or missing guidance can be reviewed quickly."

Show:

- Programs module.
- Feedback module.
- Voice input/read-aloud button in chatbot.

## Demo recording notes

- Lead with impact before showing UI details.
- Do not spend too long on navigation.
- Avoid saying the app decides eligibility.
- Say "may match" and "verify with official office."
- End on the human verification workflow.
