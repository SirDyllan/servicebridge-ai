# ServiceBridge AI Submission Pack

This folder contains the materials needed to prepare the hackathon submission and demo narrative.

## Project

ServiceBridge AI helps people understand possible public support pathways, prepare documents, and verify next steps with official offices or qualified human advisers.

## Core impact story

Before ServiceBridge AI, a user with food, school, or ID-document problems may not know which program name to search, which documents matter, or which office should verify the case.

After ServiceBridge AI, the user can explain the situation in plain language, see possible support pathways, prepare a document checklist, and move toward a human or official verification route.

## Submission files

- `DEMO_VIDEO_SCRIPT.md` - 2.5 minute demo structure based on mentor guidance.
- `PITCH_NARRATIVE.md` - written pitch language for the project.
- `RESPONSIBLE_AI.md` - responsible AI choices, limits, and human role.
- `SUBMISSION_CHECKLIST.md` - final pre-submit checklist.

## Demo focus

Do not only show screens. Show the change for the user:

1. Confusion before: "I need help with food and school expenses, and I do not have an ID."
2. ServiceBridge AI turns that into possible support pathways.
3. The user receives documents to prepare and next steps.
4. The app routes final eligibility and requirements to human or official verification.

## Suggested live scenario

Use this example:

> I am 18, I am a student in Mutare City, I lost my part-time job, and I need help with food and school expenses. I do not have an ID yet. I need help this week.

Expected demo outcome:

- Food support and student/education support may appear.
- Zimbabwe or local document readiness may appear if ID is missing.
- The result cards show documents and next steps directly.
- The human verification module explains where to verify requirements.
