# Responsible AI

## Product boundary

ServiceBridge AI is a benefits navigation and preparation tool. It is not an approval system.

The app can:

- Suggest possible public support pathways.
- Explain why a pathway may fit.
- Show documents to prepare.
- Highlight missing or unknown documents.
- Route users toward official or human verification.

The app must not:

- Say the user is approved.
- Guarantee eligibility.
- Invent addresses, phone numbers, deadlines, or program rules.
- Replace official agencies, schools, social workers, case workers, or qualified advisers.

## Human role

Final eligibility and approval remain with official agencies, schools, social workers, case workers, or verified support providers.

The human verification module exists to make that boundary visible and actionable.

## Source transparency

Result cards show:

- Source label.
- Program or pathway name.
- Verification status.
- Last verified date when available.

If a source URL is missing, the app shows a review note instead of a broken button.

## Voice feature note

Voice input and read-aloud are accessibility features. Users should review dictated text before sending it. Browser speech recognition availability depends on the user's device and browser.

## Safety language

Use:

- "May match"
- "Possible support pathway"
- "Prepare these documents"
- "Verify with the official office"

Avoid:

- "You qualify"
- "You are approved"
- "This office will definitely help"
- "These requirements are guaranteed"
