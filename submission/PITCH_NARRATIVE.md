# Pitch Narrative

## One-liner

ServiceBridge AI turns a confusing support need into possible benefit pathways, document readiness, and a safe human verification route.

## Problem

People often do not describe their problem using official program names. They say things like:

"I need help with food and school expenses, and I do not have an ID."

That single sentence can involve food support, education support, emergency relief, document readiness, and human verification. Without guidance, users may prepare the wrong documents, visit the wrong office, or give up before reaching support.

## Solution

ServiceBridge AI lets users describe their situation naturally. It returns:

- Possible support matches.
- Why each pathway may fit.
- Documents to prepare.
- Missing documents to check.
- Next 3 steps.
- Source and verification information.
- A human or official verification route.

## Impact

Before: the user has stress, uncertainty, and no clear path.

After: the user has a checklist, possible pathways, source transparency, and a safer next step toward a real human or official office.

## Responsible AI position

ServiceBridge AI does not approve benefits, make final eligibility decisions, or replace official agencies. It uses possible-match language and keeps the human role visible.

## Why this matters

The product helps users prepare before they apply or visit an office. It reduces confusion, supports document readiness, and makes the transition from AI guidance to human verification clearer.
